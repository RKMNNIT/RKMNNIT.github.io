About Me

Rakesh Rai is a dedicated researcher, educator, and data enthusiast with a strong background in computational intelligence, analytics, and neuroscience-inspired artificial intelligence. He completed his B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from NIT Nagaland and worked as a Data Analyst at IGL for three years, where he gained industry experience in large-scale data analysis and insight generation.

He is currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at KIIT University, Bhubaneswar, while pursuing his PhD in Computer Science at MNNIT Allahabad. His research focuses on human cognitive behavior analysis and brain state modeling using EEG signals, exploring how physiological and affective conditions influence neural dynamics through advanced deep learning frameworks.
