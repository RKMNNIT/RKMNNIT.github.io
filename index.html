About Me

Rakesh Rai is a dedicated researcher, educator, and data enthusiast with a strong background in technology, analytics, and computational neuroscience. He completed his B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from NIT Nagaland and worked as a Data Analyst at IGL for three years.

He is currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the CSE Department at KIIT University, Bhubaneswar, while pursuing his PhD in Computer Science at MNNIT Allahabad. His research focuses on Human Cognitive Behavior and Brain State Analysis using EEG signals, exploring how physiological conditions influence brain activity.
