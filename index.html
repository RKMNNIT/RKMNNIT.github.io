About Me

Rakesh Kumar Rai is an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Engineering at KIIT University, Bhubaneswar, and a PhD Scholar in Computer Science at Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology (MNNIT), Allahabad.

His research focuses on EEG signal analysis and computational neuroscience, particularly the development of deep learning frameworks for cognitive state detection and neurological disorder analysis. His work explores sleep stage classification, Alzheimer’s disease detection, and brain–computer interfaces using advanced deep learning and explainable AI techniques.

Research Interests

  • Sleep Stage Classification
  • Brain–Computer Interface (BCI)
  • Alzheimer’s Disease Detection
